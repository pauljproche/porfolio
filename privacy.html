Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how ICEBURG collects, uses, and protects the personal information of its website visitors and users.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit our website or contact us, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and any additional information you provide voluntarily.

2. Use of Collected Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Send you updates, newsletters, and promotional materials with your consent.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Protect against fraudulent or unauthorized activities.

3. Information Sharing

We believe in the value of privacy and will not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, unless required by law.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the personal information we collect. However, we understand that privacy is a fundamental right, and we strive to exceed industry standards to ensure the security and confidentiality of your data.

5. Respect for User Control

We respect your autonomy and believe in empowering individuals to have control over their personal information. We provide options for you to manage your preferences, including the ability to opt out of certain communications or requests for information.

6. Transparency and Accountability

We are committed to being transparent about our data practices and accountable for how we handle your personal information. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please don't hesitate to contact us.
